Research

Deep learning, Vision, AI for Science and Society

My main research area is computer vision—understanding our world using visual data with the help of state-of-the-art deep learning models. I am particularly passionate about understanding objects in diverse and complex environments using images and videos.

I believe A.I. can and should be developed to improve people's quality of life. I am actively collaborating with researchers across disciplines to apply A.I. in scientific discovery, healthcare advancement, and societal challenges, aiming to create meaningful impact beyond traditional computer vision applications.
